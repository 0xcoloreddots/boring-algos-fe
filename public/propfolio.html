FAQ

Your questions, answered.

How many strategies are in each portfolio?+
Each portfolio contains multiple strategies selected for low correlation, typically between 3 and 6 per portfolio. Each strategy file (ex5) comes with 2 license keys, meaning you can run that strategy on 2 different broker accounts simultaneously. The 3 portfolios contain no overlapping strategies, so your keys across portfolios are completely independent of each other.
Which prop firms does it work with?+
Propfolio is optimised against standard prop firm challenge rules covering drawdown limits, profit targets, and time windows. It works with major prop firms that allow automated trading (EAs). During your onboarding call we confirm compatibility with your specific firm and flag any rule differences to be aware of. Both 1-stage and 2-stage firms are supported.
Do I need coding experience?+
No. You need to understand what a prop firm challenge is, what drawdown means, and what a positive-expectancy system looks like. You don't need to code anything. The team walks you through the full setup on a live call. Most members are live within 24 hours of joining.
How much time does it take each week?+
Setup takes 30–60 minutes on the setup call. After that the system runs fully automatically 24/7. Most members spend 5–10 minutes a day checking that everything is running. That's it.
What happens if the algos fail a challenge?+
Failing one challenge does not mean the system doesn't work. Trading is expectancy, not certainty. Our simulations show an 80%+ pass rate, meaning roughly 1 in 5 attempts won't pass first time. That's statistically expected. The guarantee means we keep working with you at no extra charge until you receive a payout that covers your membership cost. We review what happened, adjust if needed, and run the next challenge with you.

No — and here's why that's intentional. Open-source strategies get replicated and arbitraged, which degrades their edge faster. The black-box model protects the portfolio's performance over time. You can also see full backtest reports inside PropfolioOS before you commit to running anything.
